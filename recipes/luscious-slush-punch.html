Luscious Slush Punch

Original Link

Description

This slush recipe makes the best non-alcoholic punch that both kids and adults enjoy. Makes enough for two bowls. This is our Christmas Eve tradition and there is never a drop left!

Ingredients

  • 6 cups water
  • 2 ½ cups white sugar
  • 2 (3 ounce) packages strawberry flavored Jell-O mix
  • 1 (46 fluid ounce) can pineapple juice
  • ⅔ cup lemon juice
  • 1 quart orange juice
  • 2 (2 liter) bottles lemon-lime flavored carbonated beverage

Steps

  1. Bring water, sugar, and strawberry-flavored gelatin to a boil in a large saucepan; boil for 3 minutes. Stir in pineapple juice, lemon juice, and orange juice.

  2. Divide mixture into two separate containers and freeze.

  3. Combine contents of 1 container frozen juice mixture with 1 bottle lemon-lime flavored carbonated beverage in a punch bowl; stir until slushy. Repeat with remaining portions as needed.
